Exams

Create exams from your own study material with AI. Practice, review your results and improve the way you study.

About Exams

Exams is a mobile app designed to help students and learners generate practice exams from their own content. Add your study material, choose how you want to practice, and let the app create questions to help you prepare more efficiently.

How It Works

1. Add Your Content

Use your own notes, documents or study material as the base for your exam.

2. Generate Questions

Create custom practice exams with AI based on the content you provide.

3. Practice and Review

Answer questions, check your results and learn from your mistakes.

Main Features

  • AI-powered exam generation
  • Create exams from your own study material
  • Customizable practice sessions
  • Multiple question formats
  • Results and review after each exam
  • Useful for students, teachers and self-learners

Designed for Smarter Study

For Students

Prepare for tests and exams using your own notes and materials.

For Self-Learners

Turn any topic into a structured practice session.

For Teachers

Use AI-generated questions as a starting point for learning activities or assessments.

Study better with AI

Exams helps you transform study material into practice questions, making revision faster and more active.
